World wide web

The World wide web (WWW) is a system of interlinked hypertext documents accessed via the Internet. It is a service that runs on top of the Internet.

Since the 1990's, the world wide web has transformed how we live, work and interact. It has had a profound impact on our lives since then. Technology is moving toward web-based applications that let us perform tasks such as managing our money, booking travels and even purchasing goods and services without having to step away from the computer.
