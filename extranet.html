Extranet

An extranet is a network that provides controlled access to an organizations intranet for external users such as customers, suppliers, or business partners.

It serves as a bridge between the internal network and the external world, allowing secure collaboration and data sharing.

  • Provides limited external access
  • Supports business collaboration
  • Maintains security and control

Extranets are commonly used in supply chain management and partnerships where sharing information securely is essential.
